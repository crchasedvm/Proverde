Advanced Gut Health for Thriving Animals

Proverde is an all-natural, highly concentrated blend of hydrolyzed yeast and yeast cell wall, designed to enhance animal health and productivity by supporting gut integrity and immunity.

Core Benefits of Proverde

Effective Pathogen Binding

Contains Mannan Oligosaccharides (MOS) that bind to harmful pathogens like E. coli and Salmonella, preventing them from attaching to the gut lining.

Robust Immune Support

Rich in Beta-glucans that modulate the immune system, preparing it to respond effectively to challenges and supporting long-term antibody responses.

Supports Rapid Tissue Growth

Provides a rich source of readily available nucleotides, the essential building blocks for DNA in rapidly growing tissues like the intestines of young animals.

Proven Efficacy Through Research

Binding Effectiveness Against Pathogens

Proverde has been proven to bind 95% to 100% of various strains of E. coli and Salmonella, effectively reducing the risk of enteric infections.

Inhibition of Cryptosporidium parvum

In-vitro research shows Proverde effectively inhibits the binding of C. parvum sporozoites, a common cause of diarrhea in calves. At a concentration of 60 mg/mL, it achieved 72.47% binding inhibition.

Compositional Comparison

Proverde consistently shows higher concentrations of key active ingredients, MOS and Beta-glucan, when compared to other products in the market.
